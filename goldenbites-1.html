Our Story

Where Culture Meets Flavour

Golden Bites was born from a simple love: the incredible food of Brazil and the Caribbean. Founded by Marcus Silva, we've been bringing the warmth, colour and soul of these vibrant cuisines to Birmingham since 2018.

Every dish tells a story — from slow-cooked feijoada passed down through generations, to jerk chicken marinated in family recipes brought straight from Jamaica. We cook with heart, and you taste the difference.
